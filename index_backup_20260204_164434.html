Respect Devs

Dont ask developers for free code. Respect their time, skills, and boundaries.

Clear asks. Fair budgets. Better outcomes.

Wrong

Hey bro can you code me a cheat for free?
Can you just make my whole website?
I have an idea, you code it, we split profits.

Better

Hi, I would like to hire you for a small project.
Could you point me to resources where I can learn this myself?
I have a budget and would love your help.

Also

Share goals, budget range, and deadlines up front.
Agree on scope before asking for estimates.
Respect focus time and communication windows.

Developers are people too. Respect their work.

Hire fairly. Collaborate honestly.
