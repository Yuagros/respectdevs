Respect Devs

Dont expect free work from developers. Value their time and expertise.

Avoid Dont say

  • Can you build my app for free?
  • I just need a tiny favor, can you code this quickly?
  • I have an idea, you do the work, and well figure out profits later.

Better Try this

  • Hi, Im looking for professional help and Im happy to pay for it.
  • Could you suggest learning resources so I can try it myself?
  • I have a small project and would love your guidance.

Developers deserve respect. Always honor their time and skills.
