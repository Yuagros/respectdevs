Respect Devs

Value the craft. Clear requests, fair budgets, and honest timelines build better products and better partnerships.

Avoid The red flags

  • Can you build my app for free?
  • I just need a tiny favor, can you code this quickly?
  • I have an idea, you do the work, we will split later.

Better The respect path

  • I am looking for professional help and I can pay for it.
  • Could you suggest learning resources so I can try it myself?
  • I have a small project and would love your guidance.

Tip Set the tone

  • Share goals, budget range, and deadlines up front.
  • Agree on scope before asking for estimates.
  • Protect focus time and respect boundaries.
Developers deserve respect. Honor their time and skills, and you will get better work in return.
Respectful collaboration builds stronger products.
